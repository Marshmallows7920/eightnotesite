About Eighth Note

Eighth Note, the music journaling app, will allow music-lovers to keep track of and analyze songs they listen to, their thoughts, and the memories associated with them. Offering a personal and reflective space to explore their emotional connection to music, the journal makes it easy for music fans to capture how certain tracks resonate with them. They can attach personal notes, reflections, or memories to each song they log. Music-lovers can search for songs they've journaled about by title, artist, and genre, and create more entries for that song if they wish. For added personalization and organization, journalers can create custom playlists that group songs and journal entries in any way they choose.
