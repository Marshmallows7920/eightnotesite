What is Eighth Note?

Eighth Note, the music journaling app, will allow music-lovers to keep track of and analyze songs they listen to, their thoughts, and the memories associated with them. Offering a personal and reflective space to explore their emotional connection to music, the journal makes it easy for music fans to capture how certain tracks resonate with them. They can attach personal notes, reflections, or memories to each song they log.

Music-lovers will be able to tag songs with moods or genres to further express their emotional connection and organize their entries. For added personalization and organization, journalers can create custom playlists that group songs and journal entries in any way they choose.

What is its purpose?

Eighth Note will provide music enthusiasts with the ability to browse or search their journal entries by date, mood, genre or song title, making it easy to revisit their past entries and rediscover songs they listened to in the past. There will also be functionality for journal users to look back at a specific day, month, or year to reflect on the music they enjoyed during that period in their life.
Music-lovers will also have access to information about their listening habits in general, including breakdowns of genres and artists listened to. These metrics will also be available for journalers to download as an infographic or report, so they can share what they‚Äôve been listening to with friends and family.

The app will be a significant improvement over other journaling mediums, with the added ability to organize songs and search for entries without flipping through numerous pages. Unlike physical journals, our digital song journal will never be lost or damaged so journal users can keep their entries and musical memories indefinitely. Eighth Note will also appeal to those who are looking to expand and explore their music taste, by being able to see music that is popular among other users. Additionally, with the overview of user listening habits available, music-lovers will have a clear view of how their music taste is evolving, a feature not present in physical journals. Overall, journalers will feel more inspired, will have a significantly better user experience, and can more easily access and revisit their memories with our song journaling app.

Who is it for?

Designed for music-lovers, this app will provide them with a nostalgic way to look back on previous memories and feelings through music. The app will be used by people who tend to find personal meaning in the songs they listen to, and want to remember when and why a song resonated with them. Journalers can discuss the memories or people that the song reminds them of, and explain or tag a song with a mood they associate with it.
With the ability to embed certain lyrics from the song within their journal entry, reflective listeners can easily capture how and why the song is important to them.

When and where are you guys in operation?

By combining journaling and music, Eighth Note will help music-lovers build an archive of meaningful songs and memories. Eighth Note will be considered successful if journal users prefer the app over traditional journaling options and find the app intuitive, which can be measured through surveys. We would consider this criteria met if at least 80% of app users who fill out surveys indicate that they feel this way. With the added ability for journalers to keep track of how many days in a row they have engaged with the journal, we can use this feature to gauge how frequently music-lovers are logging into the app with the hope that 50% of them journal 3 times a week.
